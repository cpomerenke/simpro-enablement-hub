The golden rule
Timesheet lodgement and approval happens in either Simpro or EH HR — never both for the same employee. EH Payroll always receives the approved data and runs the pay.

What does each platform answer?

Core distinction
SIMPRO

"Who is doing which job, where — and what did that labour cost the job?"

  • Job-linked scheduling & dispatch
  • Field tech time capture (Simpro Mobile)
  • Timesheet approval by operations supervisor
  • Job cost actuals & profitability reporting
  • Links time to billable jobs & work orders
EH HR PLATFORM

"Who is rostered for which shift — and have they lodged their timesheet?"

  • Shift rostering for fixed-site or shift staff
  • Employee timesheet lodgement (EH Work App / Hero Time Clock)
  • Manager timesheet approval within EH HR
  • Shift bidding, swapping & availability
  • Passes approved timesheets to EH Payroll
EH PAYROLL

"What do we owe each employee this pay period — and is it award-compliant?"

  • Receives approved timesheets from EH HR or Simpro (SyncEzy)
  • Modern Award Pay Conditions Engine
  • Pay run — gross wages, PAYG, deductions
  • STP Phase 2 reporting to ATO
  • Superannuation processing (Payday Super)

Platform deep dive

What each platform owns
📅

Simpro: Scheduling

Dispatches field techs to specific customer jobs. Manages work orders, job sections, and cost centres. The only platform that links time to a billable job.

📱

Simpro Mobile

Field technicians clock on/off tied to a job card. GPS tracking, job status updates, materials capture. Time is coded against job number automatically.

🔗

SyncEzy Integration

Locked Simpro timesheets flow to EH Payroll. This is the bridge between Simpro's job-costing world and EH Payroll's award compliance engine.

🗓

EH HR: Rostering

Builds shift patterns for fixed-site or shift-based staff. Employees view and confirm shifts via EH Work App. No concept of a "job" — only shifts at locations.

⏱

Hero Time Clock

Tablet-based clock-in at fixed work sites. Feeds time into EH HR as a timesheet. Manager confirms in EH HR before data moves to Payroll.

✅

EH HR: Approvals

Managers approve timesheets within EH HR. Shift bidding, swapping, and availability management. Award cost visibility per shift before publishing.

⚖️

EH Payroll: Award Engine

Receives approved timesheets from either Simpro (SyncEzy) or EH HR (native). Applies legally binding Modern Award rules — penalty rates, overtime, allowances. Executes the pay run: gross wages, PAYG, deductions. STP Phase 2, superannuation (Payday Super), payslip generation. This is the payroll system of record for all staff.

⚠️
Key distinction
Simpro schedule rate multipliers (1.5x, 2x) are for job-costing only — not for legal wage calculation. EH Payroll's Pay Conditions Engine applies the legally binding Modern Award rules when processing wages.

EH Work App

Simpro Only: Job Scheduling & Timesheets

Field techs clock on/off against job cards. Timesheets approved in Simpro, then sent to EH Payroll for wage processing. EH HR exists for people management but rostering is not activated.

Best fit when...

  • ✓Every labour hour must be costed against a specific job/quote
  • ✓Techs move between 3–6 short service calls per day
  • ✓Business invoices clients based on hours spent on site
  • ✓Job profitability reporting is critical
  • ✓Scheduling is reactive — no fixed roster is possible
  • ✓Staff need GPS and location tracking per job

Not a fit when...

  • ✗Staff work predictable, repeating shift patterns
  • ✗Modern Award penalty rate compliance is the primary concern
  • ✗More than 20% casual employees with variable hours
  • ✗Work is measured by site attendance, not job completion

Industries

Electrical Plumbing HVAC Fire protection Security services Mechanical
Example: Metro Electrical Services, Sydney NSW

22 licensed electricians across residential and commercial work.

Scheduling: Each morning, scheduler dispatches techs from the Simpro calendar. A tech might do a fault-find at 7:30am, a switchboard quote at 10am, and a solar installation in the afternoon — three jobs, three customers.

Time capture: Each tech clocks on via Simpro Mobile on arrival. Clock-off is automatic when they submit the job card. Time is coded against the job number and cost centre automatically.

Approval: Week-end, the operations manager reviews and locks timesheets in Simpro Premium.

Payroll: Approved timesheets flow to EH Payroll. Electrical Trades Award applied automatically. EH HR used for onboarding, leave, and performance — not timesheets.

EH HR Rostering + EH Payroll (No Simpro)

Staff work predictable, repeating shifts. Rosters built in EH HR. Employees lodge timesheets via EH Work App or Hero Time Clock. Approved data flows natively to EH Payroll.

Facilities management Commercial cleaning Security guarding Hospitality support Workshop / fabrication

95 cleaners across 18 fixed commercial sites.

Rostering: Site managers build weekly rosters in EH HR by building. Cleaners view and confirm shifts via the EH Work App.

Time capture: Cleaners clock in via tablets running Hero Time Clock at each site.

Approval: Site managers approve timesheets in EH HR.

Payroll: Approved data flows natively to EH Payroll. Cleaning Services Award applied — all penalty rates automated. No Simpro involvement. Work measured by site attendance, not job completion.

Key rule

  • ✓Two separate approval paths — one payroll destination
  • ✓No employee submits timesheets in both systems
  • ✓Field staff: Simpro Mobile → Simpro approval → EH Payroll
  • ✓Fixed-site staff: EH Work App → EH HR approval → native
  • ✓EH Payroll consolidates all streams in one pay run

Common profile

  • ✓Electrical or mechanical businesses with a workshop
  • ✓Trade businesses that also run depot/admin staff
  • ✓Fire & security with both field techs and site staff
  • ✓50–300 employees across mixed role types
Example: Allied Mechanical Services, Brisbane QLD

45 field techs + 12 workshop fabricators.

Field: Simpro dispatching, Simpro Mobile clock-on, weekly lock → EH Payroll.

Workshop: EH HR rostering (three shifts), Hero Time Clock on floor, manager approves in EH HR → native to EH Payroll.

Manufacturing Award penalty rates automated. All 57 staff in one EH Payroll pay run per fortnight. Written policy: field labour owned by Simpro, fixed-shift owned by EH HR.

Integration ROI — Why a third-party partner pays for itself

Connecting Simpro to Employment Hero via a specialist integration partner (Aloka or Syncezy) is not an additional cost — it's a return-generating investment. Here's the business case.

The cost of doing nothing

Manual process risk
4–6 hrs
Lost every pay cycle to manual data re-entry between Simpro and payroll. At 26 pay cycles per year, that's 104–156 hours of admin time per year — the equivalent of 3–4 full working weeks.
$50K+
Average Fair Work underpayment penalty for a single award non-compliance breach. Manual timesheet re-entry creates transcription errors that misapply penalty rates, overtime, and allowances — each one a compliance liability.
3–5 days
Typical delay between a field technician completing work in Simpro and that time being accurately reflected in payroll — causing payroll disputes, correction runs, and employee dissatisfaction.
💡
The core argument
A third-party integration partner doesn't just connect two systems — they eliminate the human error layer between job completion and payroll processing. The integration fee is recovered within 1–2 pay cycles through time savings alone. Compliance risk reduction is the compounding return on top.

Value drivers
⏱

Admin Time Recovered

Each automated pay cycle eliminates 4–6 hours of manual re-entry. A 50-person field team running fortnightly payroll recovers over 130 admin hours per year — equivalent to a part-time hire.

⚖️

Compliance Risk Eliminated

Every manual transcription is a potential underpayment. With the integration, EH Payroll's award engine interprets Simpro timesheet data directly — no human interpretation, no exposure.

🔐

Certification-Gated Dispatch

Aloka's integration blocks dispatch of any technician whose licence or certification has lapsed in EH HR. One avoided compliance incident covers years of integration fees.

📅

Scheduling Accuracy

Real-time leave status in Simpro means dispatchers never accidentally schedule staff who are on leave. Fewer failed jobs, fewer customer calls, fewer rescheduling costs.

👤

Onboarding Speed

New starters onboarded in EH HR flow directly to Simpro — no double-entry. Technicians are dispatch-ready from day one, reducing the onboarding lag that delays productive billing.

📊

Single Source of Truth

One integrated data pipeline from job clock-on to payslip. Audits, disputes, and reporting take minutes instead of days. Finance and HR speak the same numbers.
